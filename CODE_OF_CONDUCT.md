# Code of Conduct

We want MVNG Consulting repositories to be welcoming, professional, and productive spaces for everyone who contributes.

## Our standards

- **Be respectful.** Treat people and ideas with respect, even when disagreeing.
- **Be constructive.** Give and receive feedback with the goal of improving the work.
- **Be inclusive.** Welcome contributors regardless of background, experience, or role.
- **Be professional.** Communicate clearly, avoid harassment, and keep discussions focused on the work.

Unacceptable behaviour includes intimidation, discrimination, harassment, personal attacks, trolling, or sustained disruption of discussion.

## Reporting issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, contact us at **hello@mvng.nz**. All reports will be handled confidentially.

## Scope

This code applies to all MVNG Consulting repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, discussions, and any project space where we collaborate.
